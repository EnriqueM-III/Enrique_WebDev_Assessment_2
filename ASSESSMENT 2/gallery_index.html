These are some of my works I have created. I have commissioned more than 50 artworks and these few I selected are I chose to be displayed on my portfolio.

A little background as to what these are; I made these as a part of a Text-based Roleplaying Community where people partake in writing activities while roleplaying as characters in a fictional setting. In this case, a fantasy world where mages carry these magical books that grant them abilities. These books are called Grimoires.

The community is named Rise of the Zodiacs. The lore is loosely based on the famous manga and anime series, Black Clover. Having their own original story and worldbuilding. The grimoires created by members of the community contain a symbol. The symbol varies on the kingdom the character resides in (Clover, Diamond, Heart, Spade) The clover variant however, their rarity and power varies on the number of clovers on the symbol.

(3 = Regular, 4 = Luck, 5 = Cursed)

Aside from being a member of this community and writing alongside them. I was happy to be involved with the community to add something special to their characters. I created covers of their grimoires.

I am simply given a material as to what their character's abilities are, specific symbols, color scheme. etc. to work with and show them the results. Making use of editing programs along with saved images to work on, I was able to create book covers that compliment their descriptions.

Every one that commissioned a piece were more than satisfied with the results and it is an overall amazing experience for me as an aspiring creator.
